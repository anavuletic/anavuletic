21 hours refers to the 21 years of life we have experienced so far and 21 mass shootings that changed the world while we experienced it. Our purpose was to capture these moments of horror in time similar to how clocks stop after a nuclear explosion. Using a minimalistic setup, each clock states the exact time a victim was shot over the past 21 years while the red clock displays our current time to symbolize that if we don’t make a significant change in our arms rights than a tragedy just like one of these may occur at any moment. Our current time clock is red because red signifies the warning and overall horror of the thought of these actions repeating. Recently events similar to the ones we’ve displayed have been witnessed more frequently and if there isn’t a change in our laws these tragedies will continue to take place. Therefore it is up to us, as we will be the future politicians and leaders, to make a difference in our society and stop the clock.
